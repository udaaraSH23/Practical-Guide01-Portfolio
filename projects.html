Flood Prediction and Warning System

The Flood Alert App is a user-friendly mobile application designed to provide timely alerts and essential information during flood emergencies. Developed as a creative design group project in the second year, second semester, this app integrates real-time water level monitoring, emergency contact features, and sensor technology for effective flood management. This App ensures user safety with several critical features. Real-time alerts provide instant notifications when water levels exceed safe limits, prompting timely precautions. User registration allows users to register their mobile numbers and locations to receive localized alerts. The emergency contacts feature offers quick access to important numbers, including local authorities and rescue services. Additionally, sensor integration connects with deployed sensors in flood-prone areas, delivering real-time water level data directly to the app for the most current updates.
